Privacy Policy.

This is the privacy policy for Cybersmith Ltd and describes the personal data we process.

This document is a simple description of our activities, it does not replace and is secondary to all contracts we may have with your organisation.

If you wish to contact us regarding a privacy matter please see our contact page.

The Data We Process.

As a business to business organisation we do not collect data from members of the general public.

If you are a customer the data we process and how it is stored will be included in the contract we have with you.

If you are a potential customer we electronically store your information for marketing and quoting purposes.

Our Lawful Basis For Data Processing.

The data we process for our customers is done to meet a contractual obligation to provide them with services.

The data we process for our potential customers is done with either that customer's consent or as part of our legitimate interest to market to other businesses.

How To Complain.

If you have any concerns about our use of your personal information, you can make a complaint to us at info@cybersmith.co.uk.

You can also complain to the ICO if you are unhappy with how we have used your data.
