Cyber Assurance.

What Is It.

Cyber Assurance (CA) a scheme run by IASME, the body that also runs Cyber Essentials (CE). It builds on CE to further secure your systems from a wider range of attacks.

Just like CE it comes in two stages. First you fill in a self-assessment questionnaire, then an assessor tests your systems.
